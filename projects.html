BlackOakArkansas.Net

From mid 2018 until September 2021, I had the extreme pleasure of working with Rickie Lee "Richochet" Reynolds as Webmaster on his website BlackOakArkansas.Net.

The first time I saw Black Oak Arkansas was in 1971. I later met Rickie in person for the first time in Memphis during the late 1980s and at the Tuppalo, Mississippi reunion concernt in 2003.

The site, a WordPress site with custom ecommerce code, closed shortly after Rickie was hospitalized with Covid-19 and subsequently died.
