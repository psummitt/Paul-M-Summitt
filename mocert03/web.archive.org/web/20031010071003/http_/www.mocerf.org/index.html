The County Employees' Retirement Fund Proudly Announces the 2003 Savings Plan Matching Contribution

CERF's Board of Directors announced at its June meeting that the full Savings Plan match allowed by law is going to be offered again this year.
The Board has determined that CERF will match funds deposited into the 457 portion of the Savings Plan* for the year 2003 as follows:
For Non-LAGERS Members
50¢ on the dollar, up to the first 6% of pay
For LAGERS Members
25¢ on the dollar, up to the first 6% of pay
To be eligible to receive the matching contribution from CERF, you must work at least 1,000 hours during the calendar year and remain employed through December 31, 2003.  The matching contribution will be added to eligible accounts during the first quarter of 2004.
* The matching contribution will not be applied to money transferred from a previous deferred compensation plan.

NOTICE

Until final salary and hours information through termination date is received from the County Clerk on an employee ready to retire, the CERF Administrative Office cannot commence retirement benefits. However, benefits will be paid retroactively to the day the retiree is entitled.

Just a Reminder...

Though an employee who makes contributions will be terminating employment, DO NOT stop the payroll deduction for this employee's last pay period. As required by law, contributions MUST continue to be made through the date of termination.

CERF 457 Plan Hits Milestone...

The number of members with a balance in the CERF 457 plan recently hit 2500.  Since the plan began 1/1/2000, participation has grown every year despite some tough market conditions.  Every county within the CERF system has at least one member participating in the plan.
